{% docs mrt_sales_localbike__customer_segments %}

This mart segments customers based on their total revenue and purchasing behavior.

It includes:
- **customer_id**, **customer_email**, **city**, **zipcode**: Customer details.
- **number_of_orders**: Total orders placed.
- **total_revenue**: Total amount spent.
- **avg_order_value**: Average value per order.
- **customer_segment**: Segment label (Low, Medium, High Value).

Useful for targeted marketing, upselling, and retention campaigns.
